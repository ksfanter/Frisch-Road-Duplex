Application Process

To apply for one of these houses, please follow these steps:

  1. Download the application form from our website.
  2. How to Apply
  3. Fill out the form with your personal information.
  4. Submit the completed form via email to frisch@fanter.io.
  5. Wait for a confirmation email regarding your application status.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at frisch@fanter.io
